## blast / zlib

Source: <https://github.com/madler/zlib/tree/master/contrib/blast>

License: zlib.

The Setup Factory 7 PKWARE implode decoder is an altered, bounded C# implementation derived from the public `blast` algorithm. It validates headers, Huffman codes, back-references, end markers, and expanded-output limits. No external decoder is invoked.
